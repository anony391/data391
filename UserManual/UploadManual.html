Uploading Manual

This module will be used by radiologists to first enter a radiology record, and then to upload medical images into the radiology record. A radiology record can be created without uploading an image, and additional images can be added later to a record.

    Create radiology record:

  1. Select “Create New Radiology Records” in home menu
  2. Enter the ID of the patient
  3. Enter the ID of the doctor in charge of the patient with ID entered in step 1
  4. Enter the ID of the radiologist creating the record
  5. Enter the test type
  6. Enter the prescribing and test date
  7. Enter the diagnosis and description of the diagnosis
  8. Press “Choose Files” to upload images. You can choose not to upload any images.
  9. Press “Submit” to create radiology record and/or upload an image

    Upload an image:

  1. Select “Upload Images to Record” in home menu
